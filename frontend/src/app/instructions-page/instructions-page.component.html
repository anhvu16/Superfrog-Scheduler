You're on your way to an awesome event!

Here's some info you might need to know before making a request.

Requesting

You'll choose your event date from our calendar of available dates. Any event must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance.

Pricing

Superfrogs cost $100 an hour for private events, and $75 an hour for TCU events. Showgirls and Cheerleaders are an added cost, that changes on a case by case basis.
