You're on your way to an awesome event!

Here's some info you might need to know before making a request.

Requesting

You'll choose your event date from our calendar of available dates. Any event must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance.

Range

We can only service requests that are within a 100 mile radius from Amon G. Carter Stadium.

Pricing

SuperFrogs cost $175 an hour for private events, and $100 an hour for Public School, Non-profit, and TCU events. Showgirls and Cheerleaders are an added cost.
